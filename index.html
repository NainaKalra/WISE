About WISE

Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) is a student organization at Indiana Tech dedicated to supporting and empowering women pursuing careers in STEM. Through mentorship, collaborative projects, workshops, and networking opportunities, WISE creates a welcoming community.

What Makes Us Special

Networking

Building strong connections with industry professionals.

Mentorship

Guidance from seniors and faculty to navigate STEM.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions to master technical tools.

At WISE, the members lead the way. Every month, WISE members choose their own theme for upcoming events!
